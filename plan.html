Mission Statement

To increase the number of costumes produced per month & party sign-ups

Success Evaluation

Increase in Online party sign-ups.
Increase in sales of second-hand costumes.

Target Audience

There are two main target audience groups for this site.

Ages 16-25 (high schoolers & young adults): Primary target audience. The home page banner shows young cosplayers to emphasize the age-agnostic nature of cosplay.
Bright colours are used throughout the site to keep it from appearing boring.

Ages 35-50: The majority of the existing customer base falls within this age group. Pages are kept from being overly childish to avoid alienating these people.
