Target Audience

There are two main target audience groups for this site.

Ages 16-25 (high schoolers & young adults): Primary target audience. The home page banner shows young children to emphasize the lack of an age-barrier to cosplay.
Bright colours are used throughout the site to keep it from appearing boring.

Ages 35-50: The majority of the existing customer base falls within this age group.
